Our Story

How We Met

Our love story began in the most unexpected way. As teenagers, we first crossed paths at a music conference in Asante Akyem Agogo. We noticed each other, but at that time, we were far from imagining what the future held—one of us even tried to avoid the other! With no numbers exchanged, life took us back on separate paths.

But destiny had other plans. A few months later, we met again at a youth retreat. This time, something felt different. We shared numbers, conversations began, and slowly a friendship started to grow.

Then came that unforgettable day—January 1st, at the Manhyia Palace during a church program. The moment our eyes met, a spark lit between us, one that neither of us could ignore. What began as a chance encounter had blossomed into the beginning of a love that would carry us through the years.

From that day forward, our journey truly began—a journey filled with laughter, growth, faith, and love. And now, we stand on the edge of forever, ready to continue walking hand in hand into a lifetime together.

The Proposal

What began as a simple holiday became the most magical chapter of our love story. Asabed whisked me away to another city, where we explored hidden gems, created unforgettable memories, and shared laughter that filled my heart with joy.

One evening, after dinner with his friends, we strolled hand in hand by the bay, the water shimmering beneath the night sky. Ships swayed gently as if sharing our moment. Then, with city lights dancing on the waves, Asabed suddenly turned to me. With love in his eyes, he dropped to one knee and asked me to spend forever with him. My heart overflowed—tears of joy fell as I whispered yes, to the man of my dreams, my best friend, my forever.

Our Future

We're excited to start this new chapter together, building a home filled with love, laughter, and of course, lots of books. We can't wait to celebrate this special day with all of you, our dear friends and family.
